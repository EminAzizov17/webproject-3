About the Event

For our SITE 1101 course project at ADA, Team 16 hosted an "Hour of Code" event aimed at helping students explore the basics of programming and inspiring them to develop an interest in coding.

Event Details

  • Date: November 6, 2024
  • Location: Erudit Liseum
  • Number of Participants: 20 students
  • Duration: 1 hour

Activities

  1. Introduction to coding
  2. Solved creative and beginner-friendly coding tasks in an interactive format
  3. Helped students with tough exercises to enhance their understanding
  4. Conducted Q&A sessions to address questions and solve exercises

What We Taught

During the event, we used Code.org as a learning platform. Students learned the basic principles of programming through beginner-friendly puzzles.

Outcome

The event helped students solve coding puzzles, improving their problem-solving skills. All 20 students successfully completed the puzzles and earned Code.org certificates. This activity gave them a strong foundation for their future in programming.

Student Feedback

The students thoroughly enjoyed learning about programming, especially since it was their first experience with coding.
